About

Hi, I'm Leanne — a UX-led developer focused on building websites that are clear, structured, and easy to use.

I approach projects by thinking about the user first — how they move through a site, what they need to see, and how to reduce friction. From there, I design and build responsive, accessible interfaces that feel intuitive from the first interaction.

My background in visual merchandising means I naturally think in terms of layout, hierarchy, and user flow — translating business goals into experiences that guide attention and support decision-making.

I work across the full process, from wireframes in Figma to front-end development and full-stack builds in Django.

If you're looking for someone who cares about both the experience and the build quality, I'm always open to new projects.
